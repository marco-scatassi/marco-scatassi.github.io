ABOUT ME

Welcome to my website!

My name is Marco Scatassi and I'm a 23-years-old Master's student in Data Science at Milano-Bicocca University 📖. I created this page to share information about my academic background and personal interests as well as examples of projects I have worked on and tools I have used during my study path. Whether you're a fellow student, a prospective employer, or simply someone who shares my love for numbers and data, I hope you'll find something of interest here. Thanks for visiting!

MARCO SCATASSI


I have always been passionate about numbers and patterns, this led me in pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics at Bologna University 🎓. Here I could enjoy the beauty of scientific method and its complexity while having the feeling of needing something more pragmatic. Attending the courses in Computational Mathematics and Numerical Computations my interest in Computer Science increased, and I became increasingly fascinated by the power of data and mathematics to represent, approximately 😶, the real world as well as to reveal insights and drive informed decision-making. This led me to pursue a Master's degree in Data Science 📊, where I am developing new skills in data analysis, machine learning, and statistical modeling.
